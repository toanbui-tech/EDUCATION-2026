# The Journey to NAB: Engineering My Next Chapter

Reflecting on my career path as a Software Engineer, I’ve realized that growth is a deliberate choice. After over 3 years of navigating the high-speed environment of technology startups and mastering the full-stack landscape with Java, Spring Boot, and React, I am now setting a clear, ambitious sights on my next milestone: **National Australia Bank (NAB)**.

### Why NAB?
For me, NAB is the perfect intersection of technical rigor and meaningful innovation. As someone who deeply values software design principles—from the foundations of **SOLID** and **DRY** to the complexities of the **CAP Theorem**—I am drawn to an environment where software craftsmanship is the standard. NAB offers the scale and the high-availability challenges that I am eager to tackle.

### My Next Goals
As I transition toward this next chapter, my objectives are focused:
* **Engineering at Scale:** Contributing to distributed systems that serve millions of users while maintaining the highest levels of stability and performance.
* **Continuous Excellence:** Immersing myself in an elite engineering culture where I can both learn from world-class experts and contribute my proactive startup-honed problem-solving skills.
* **Innovation with Purpose:** Bridging the gap between complex financial requirements and elegant, maintainable technical solutions.

The path to NAB isn't just about a new role; it is a commitment to excellence. I am ready to push my boundaries, refine my craft, and build the future of digital banking.

**The journey continues.**
